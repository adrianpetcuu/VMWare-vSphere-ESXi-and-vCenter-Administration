## What is IT (Information Technology)

Information Technology (IT)
refers to the use of computers,
networks, software, and systems
to store, process, and manage data.

---

### Purpose of IT
- Support business operations
- Enable communication
- Manage and protect data
- Improve efficiency and productivity

---

### Core IT Areas
- **Hardware** – servers, computers, storage
- **Software** – operating systems, applications
- **Networking** – LAN, WAN, internet
- **Security** – data protection, access control
- **Support** – troubleshooting and maintenance

---

### IT Roles
- IT Support / Helpdesk
- System Administrator
- Network Administrator
- Cloud Engineer
- Security Engineer

---

### IT in Business
- Email and collaboration tools
- Web applications
- Databases and data storage
- Automation and cloud services

---

### Summary
IT is the backbone of
modern organizations, enabling
technology-driven processes,
secure data handling, and
efficient communication.
